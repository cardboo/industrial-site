About us

Rare Carat was born out of a painful experience of buying diamonds online. So we created a kick-ass product to help users search across a number of reputable sites at one go, and by using advanced data science to help intelligently select the best deal.

We hope your experience is better than ours was.

Our Mission

Seven out of eight people still buy diamonds offline. We want to bring you online to help you spend less, get more, and feel more confident doing so. We’re on your team. We’ve hand selected vendors with stellar reputations and services to search with a click.

It’s personal. Our recently engaged founder struggled with the same issues you’re having today - spending a lot of money on something he knew little about on sites he’d never heard of. Read about his journey here.

We’re also using data science and machine learning with IBM Watson to help you make the smartest decision. This blog post explains more.

In research and development, we’re testing artificial intelligence bots that ask questions in English, instead of “Diamond-ese” to help guide new buyers in finding the right stone.

If you have suggestions for features to make buying diamonds better, we’re all ears.
